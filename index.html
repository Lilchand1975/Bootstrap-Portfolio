About Me

I am Chandra Chhetri, born and brought up in Bhutan. I completed my elemantary education in Bhutan in 1990 and movedto Nepal to continue secondary and heigher secondary education. Successfully completed bachelors degree in Nepal andstarted my first profession as a teacher. I, along with my family came to America in 2008 as Refugees from Bhutan.

Initially i found it little tough to stablish new life in new country but gradually things started aligning and lifestarted being easier. I realized that i needed to go to college for better job. Joined JSR in 2010 and graduated in2015 as a bachelor in human services.

I began my first job as a case worker with Church World Services in 2015 soon after my graduation. It was a pleasantexperience to help new immigrants resettle in Richmond Virginia. I moved on to become a school liaison in early 2018 and serve the immigrant families through Commonwealth Catholic Charities.And now i am making an attempt to change my profession and go into software development through coding bootcamp through University of Richmond.
